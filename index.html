1. Task-Specific Instruction


System: You are an AI assistant specialized in summarizing scientific articles. Your summaries should be concise, accurate, and highlight the key findings and implications of the research.

User: Summarize the following abstract in 3 bullet points, focusing on the main research question, methodology, and key findings:

"Climate change poses a significant threat to global biodiversity. This study investigates the impact of rising temperatures on alpine plant species in the European Alps. Using data collected over a 20-year period from 200 mountain peaks, we employed a combination of field observations and statistical modeling to analyze changes in plant distribution and abundance. Our results show that 62% of alpine plant species have moved upslope at an average rate of 2.7 meters per decade. Furthermore, we observed a 17% decrease in species richness at lower elevations and a 31% increase at higher elevations. These findings suggest that climate change is causing a significant reorganization of alpine plant communities, with potential long-term consequences for ecosystem functioning and biodiversity conservation in mountain regions."

2. Few-Shot Learning


User: I want you to classify the sentiment of movie reviews. Here are a few examples:

Review: "This movie was absolutely fantastic! The acting was superb and the plot kept me on the edge of my seat."
Sentiment: Positive

Review: "I was really disappointed with this film. The characters were flat and the story was predictable."
Sentiment: Negative

Review: "It was an okay movie. Nothing special, but not terrible either."
Sentiment: Neutral

Now, classify the sentiment of this review:
"I can't believe how amazing this movie was! The special effects were mind-blowing and the performances were Oscar-worthy. Definitely a must-see!"

3. Chain-of-Thought Reasoning


System: You are an AI assistant capable of breaking down complex problems into step-by-step solutions. Please provide detailed reasoning for each step.

User: Solve the following word problem, showing your work and explaining each step:

"A bakery sells cookies in boxes of 12. If they have 156 cookies and want to distribute them equally among 6 charity events, how many full boxes can they give to each event, and how many cookies will be left over?"

4. Role-Playing


System: You are a seasoned journalist working for a respected news organization. Your task is to conduct an interview on a complex topic, asking probing questions and following up on the interviewee's responses.

User: I want you to role-play as a journalist interviewing a climate scientist about the potential impact of rising sea levels on coastal cities. Begin the interview with an introduction and your first question.

5. Comparative Analysis


System: You are an AI assistant capable of comparing and contrasting different concepts, products, or ideas. Please provide a detailed comparison of the following:

User: Compare and contrast the benefits and drawbacks of using a relational database management system (RDBMS) versus a NoSQL database for a large-scale e-commerce application.
